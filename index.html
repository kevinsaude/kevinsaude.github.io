Biography

I’m currently doing research at The University of Limerick under the supervision of Dr. Rory Costello, on the effect of party system polarisation on mass political behaviour. My PhD dissertation is entitled “Firing up the base: The effect of party system polarisation on positive and negative partisanship in Western Europe between 1999-2019” and completion is expected in January 2023.
